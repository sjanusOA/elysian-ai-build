Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Custom homes typically take 12-24 months, while renovations range from 3-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you work with architects and designers?

Yes! We have an in-house design team and also collaborate with external architects and designers to ensure your vision is perfectly executed.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Connecticut and surrounding areas, including New York, Massachusetts, and Rhode Island. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

Do you provide warranties?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties on all workmanship and materials. Specific warranty terms are detailed in your contract.

How do you handle permits and approvals?

We handle all necessary permits and regulatory approvals as part of our service. Our team is experienced with local building codes and requirements.

Can you work with my existing budget?

Absolutely! We work within your budget parameters and provide transparent pricing. We'll help you prioritize features to maximize your investment.
